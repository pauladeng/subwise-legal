Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

SubWise ("the App") is developed by Paula Deng ("we", "us", "our"). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data SubWise collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

None. SubWise is a fully offline application. All subscription data you enter is stored locally on your device using an on-device database (SQLite). We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

2. No Accounts or Cloud Sync

SubWise does not require you to create an account. There is no cloud sync, no login, and no server-side storage. Your data stays entirely on your device.

3. In-App Purchases

SubWise offers a one-time in-app purchase ("SubWise Pro") processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. Apple's own privacy policy governs the purchase transaction.

4. Analytics & Tracking

SubWise does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. We do not track your usage, behaviour, or location.

5. Notifications

SubWise may send local push notifications to remind you of upcoming subscription renewals. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and are never sent through an external server. You can disable them in the app's settings or in your device's notification settings at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

SubWise does not share data with any third parties. The only external network requests the app makes are:

  • Brand logos: Small icon images may be fetched from icon.horse to display subscription logos. No personal data is sent in these requests.
  • App Store: For processing in-app purchases via Apple's StoreKit framework.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all SubWise data at any time by deleting the app. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.

8. Children's Privacy

SubWise is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
